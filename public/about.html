About Me

From Teacher to Tech Builder:

Greetings! My name is Joseph, and while I may spend my days guiding future computing whizzes, I'm also on a personal mission to become a web developer myself. You see, teaching web design principles is one thing, but the thrill of actually creating on the web is something else entirely.

Beyond HTML Basics:

While my coding experience starts and ends with the HTML basics I teach 11-year-olds, my ambitions have loftier goals. I'm particularly fascinated by the potential of connecting websites to databases and building dynamic web applications. Think sleek login systems, user-friendly forms, and data flowing seamlessly – that's the magic I want to create.

Learning for Lifelong Impact:

This website reflects my journey from coding novice to (hopefully!) confident developer. As a software developer in a school setting, I believe in continuous learning and pushing boundaries. Through this project, I'm diving into linking an SQL database to a website, building login systems, and mastering form submission – all skills I plan to bring back to my workplace and share with my students.
