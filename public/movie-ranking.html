The Best Movies according to Joey

My top 3 Movies of all time!

Movie 1: Lost in Space

A family go on a trip to out of space. Little did they know that the trip would take a little longer than expected.

Movie 2: Big Momma's House

A family go on a trip to get away from the ex boyfriend who just escaped from the joint. Little did they know that their long lost big momma... was an FBI agent in disguise.

Movie 3: Big Momma's house 2

If you liked Big Momma's house 1, then this is more of the same. Pretty funny comedy staring Martin Lawrence.
